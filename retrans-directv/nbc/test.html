If you're a DIRECTV customer, you're probably frustrated - even angry - waiting for a deal to bring our channel back to your lineup.

We are frustrated, too. Very frustrated.

For months, we have tried everything we can to reach a deal. In fact, we worked through the weekend leading up to the NFL Conference Championship games in an effort to reach an agreement. But DIRECTV refused to seriously negotiate.

We have offered DIRECTV terms that are in line with those that allowed us to reach deals with other major cable and satellite companies, including two deals we've reached in just the last few months.

Sadly, DIRECTV does this all the time. Right now, DIRECTV is without more than 100 channels due to 11 different carriage battles with programmers, including TEGNA.

You don't have to wait for DIRECTV. Our stations are available on other service providers in your area.
